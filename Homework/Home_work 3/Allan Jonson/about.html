The actors of the movie "Batman" (1989)

Michael Keaton - Bruce Wayne / Batman

Michael Keaton played the main role of Bruce Wayne, known as Batman. His performance in this film has been recognized as one of the most memorable in the franchise.

Jack Nicholson — Joker

Jack Nicholson played the iconic role of Joker, the main antagonist of the film. His performance of this character became iconic in world cinema.

Kim Basinger — Vicky Vale

Kim Basinger played the role of Vicki Vale, a journalist who becomes a key figure in Bruce Wayne's story.

Michael Gough — Alfred Pennyworth

Michael Gough played the role of Bruce Wayne's faithful butler and assistant, Alfred Pennyworth.

Pat Hingle - Commissioner Gordon

Pat Gingle played the role of Commissioner Gordon, Gotham's police chief who helps Batman fight crime.

Billy Dee Williams - Harvey Dent

Billy Dee Williams played Harvey Dent, the District Attorney of Gotham, who would later become the famous supervillain Two-Face.
